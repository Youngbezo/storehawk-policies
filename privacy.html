Our commitment: StoreHawk is designed with privacy by default. We collect the minimum data necessary to provide our service and never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Introduction

StoreHawk ("we", "us", "our") operates an AI-powered dropshipping intelligence platform accessible via Telegram. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service. Please read this policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Telegram user ID and username (automatically provided by Telegram)
  • Region and market preferences you set within the bot
  • Shopify store credentials you choose to connect (stored encrypted in session)
  • Search queries and commands you submit to the bot

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Command usage logs for service improvement and debugging
  • Error logs to identify and fix technical issues
  • Session data including region preferences and connected store information

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Real name, email address, or physical address
  • Payment card or banking information
  • Private Telegram messages outside of bot commands
  • Precise location data
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses

4. TikTok Data and Platform Integrations

StoreHawk integrates with TikTok for Developers APIs. Regarding TikTok data specifically:

  • We access only publicly available TikTok trending and advertising data
  • We do not access your personal TikTok account data without explicit authorization
  • TikTok data accessed through our platform is used solely to provide product trend intelligence
  • We do not store TikTok video content on our servers
  • We do not share TikTok data with third parties
  • Data obtained via TikTok APIs is retained only for the duration of your session

Similar principles apply to our integrations with Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google, Pinterest, Snapchat, and other platforms.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: OpenAI (AI processing), Shopify (store management), SearchAPI (ad intelligence) — each bound by their own privacy policies
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Data Retention

Session data including region preferences and Shopify connections are stored in memory and cleared when the bot restarts. We do not maintain persistent user profiles beyond what is necessary to provide the Service. Server logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days for debugging purposes.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted transmission of all API credentials and sensitive data
  • Secure server hosting via Railway with access controls
  • Regular security reviews of our codebase
  • No storage of plain-text API keys or credentials in logs

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights contact us via Telegram at @StoreHawkBot.

9. Children's Privacy

StoreHawk is not directed at children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

StoreHawk serves users across 14 international markets. Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. By using the Service you consent to the transfer of your information to countries which may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

11. Cookies and Tracking

StoreHawk operates as a Telegram bot and does not use cookies or browser tracking technologies. Our web pages (storehawk-policies) do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party cookies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes via Telegram. The date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last updated. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
