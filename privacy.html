🦅 StoreHawk Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect your Telegram user ID and selected region preferences to personalise your experience. We do not collect personal information beyond what Telegram provides.

2. How We Use Information

Your data is used solely to provide StoreHawk services including product scanning, supplier finding, and store building features.

3. TikTok Data

StoreHawk accesses TikTok trending data through TikTok's official API. We display this data to users but do not store or republish TikTok content.

4. Data Storage

Session data including region preferences and Shopify connections are stored temporarily in memory and cleared on session end.

5. Third Party Services

We integrate with TikTok, Meta, Shopify, and OpenAI APIs. Each service has its own privacy policy.

6. Contact

Privacy questions? Contact us via Telegram: @StoreHawkBot
